NeoBook™ is an exclusive marketplace for college students. The platform securely facilitates transactions between students so that they can conveniently meet up on their campus to buy and sell textbooks and other items.Our platform uses Stripe Connect to seamlessly transfer payments from the buyer to the seller.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What if a buyer/seller doesn't show up?
    • If a buyer or seller doesn't show up, you can always cancel the transaction by going into the chat and clicking the top right-hand corner. You can then choose the reason for cancelling the transaction.

  • Why do I need to input the last four digits of my SSN?
    • Your SSN is needed by Stripe to verify your identity in order for you to sell something. This is required by the Know Your Customer Law, but don't worry, we do nothing else with this information.

  • Why do I have to wait to sell an item upon entering my credentials to create a Stripe account?
    • NeoBook™ prides itself on providing a secure hyperlocal marketplace for college students. As such, we take certain measures to verify the legitimacy of our sellers. Users should expect to be verified within 24 hours. Follow the link for more details https://stripe.com/docs/connect/identity-verification

  • What if the seller gives me the wrong book/item or it is not in the condition I expected?
    • If a seller shows up with the wrong book/item that does not resemble the condition in the post, you can always go into the NeoChat and cancel the transaction by clicking the top right hand corner in the respective chat.

  • How long will it take for a buyer to see their charge reflected in their bank account?
    • This will depend on the buyer's card company and bank. While Stripe sends this information right away, there may be a delay from the user's card company or bank. It is typical to see the charge reflected within 7 days.

  • How long does it take for a seller to see the money from their sale reflected in their bank account?
    • In the US, the first payment received will take 7 days to be deposited into the seller's bank account. After the first payout has been successfully deposited, the time period will be reduced to just 2 days. You can read more about bank accounts and payouts here: https://support.stripe.com/questions/bank-account-and-payouts

  • What if a buyer hasn't paid?
    • If the buyer hasn't paid, you can go into the NeoChat and request the payment. Please remember to make sure that you have been paid before you hand over your book or item.
